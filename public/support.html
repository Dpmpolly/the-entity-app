Privacy & Data Deletion

The Entity uses your Strava data solely to calculate game difficulty and track your escape progress. We do not sell your data.

How to Delete Your Data:

  1. Open The Entity App.
  2. Click the Settings (Gear) icon.
  3. Click Permanently Delete Account.

This action immediately wipes your database record and disconnects your Strava token. This cannot be undone.
