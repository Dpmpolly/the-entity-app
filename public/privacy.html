Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Welcome to The Entity ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our fitness gamification application.

1. The Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum amount of data required for the game mechanics to function. This includes:

  • Identity Information: When you sign in via Google, we collect your email address and basic profile ID to create your save file.
  • Fitness Data (via Strava): We access activity data including Distance and Start Time.
    Note: We DO NOT store GPS maps, heart rate data, or specific health biometrics. We only calculate total kilometers run.
  • Payment Information: All payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card numbers. We may store your shipping address temporarily solely for the purpose of fulfilling merchandise orders.

2. Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Machine Learning

Important for Strava Users: The marketing term "AI" used in our game refers to "Game Logic" (deterministic algorithms), not Generative AI or Machine Learning.

To comply with Strava API requirements and respect your privacy:

  • We DO NOT use your data to train, tune, or improve any Artificial Intelligence (AI) or Machine Learning (ML) models.
  • We DO NOT share your data with third-party AI companies (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or others).
  • The "Entity's" behavior is determined by a mathematical formula (Moving Average) based on your past distance inputs. It does not "learn" about you personally beyond calculating a difficulty variable.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following specific purposes:

  • Game Progression: To calculate the distance gap between you and the virtual "Entity."
  • Account Management: To verify your identity and restore your save file across devices.
  • Order Fulfillment: To send physical rewards (such as T-shirts) to your address if earned and purchased.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). We retain your game progress as long as your account is active.

Right to Deletion: You have the right to delete your data at any time. Inside the application Settings, click the "Burn Identity (Delete)" button. This will immediately and permanently erase your database records, unlink your Strava account, and remove your login credentials from our system.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app. Their privacy policies can be found below:

  • Strava: To sync running activities. (Strava Privacy Policy)
  • Google Firebase: For database hosting and authentication. (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing. (Stripe Privacy Policy)

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
